Meet Our Farmers

Buy&Cook Local cooperates with farmers living in the neighbourhood, helps farmers sell more products as well as introduce new products. Our farmers can decide themselves what products they want to offer as long as they are grown locally, do not have any pesticides and are, of course, delicious.
We take our cooperation very seriously and aim to provide high quality products and best customer service. We deliver farmer's products directly to the customer or leave them at one of our centres in the city and let customers pick up their products themselves. You do not have to worry about delivery.
Before we start any partnership we visit every farm to see how the vegetables and fruit are growing, how animals are being taken care of. You don't have to do anything, we come to you ourselves.
