About Us

We connect local farmers and people who would like to eat healthy food and learn new recipes that could be cooked from mainly regional products.

We organise regular cooking events and encourage our farmers and customers share their recipes and host cooking events, everything from the comfort of their homes.

We deliver boxes to your home or one of our local centres and encourage online payment during disturbing covid-19 times.
