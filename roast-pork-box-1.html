Roast Pork

This classic roast pork recipe with lots of delicious crackling is great for dinner with your family and friends.

prep time

20 min.

cook time

90 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : pork vegetables red wine

instructions

step 1

Rub the meat with the mustard, sprinkle with herbs and salt and lay in a roasting dish. Fry the bacon in the butter and pour over meat.

step 2

Roast in a pre-heated oven at 200 degrees C for approximately 90 minutes until juices run clear and meat is no longer pink. Periodically pour water over roast (about 3/4 cup).

step 3

In the meantime, chop the carrot, parsley and onion. Add wine and vegetables to roast after 40 minutes.

step 4

Serve with German potato dumplings (Kartoffelklößen) and German red cabbage (Rotkohl).

ingredients

4 lb boneless pork loin

3 teaspoons German mustard

1 teaspoon basil

1 teaspoon sage

1 teaspoon marjoram

2 tablespoons butter

4 strips bacon

1 carrot

1 bunch parsley

1 onion

1/2 cup red wine

tools

frying pan

oven
