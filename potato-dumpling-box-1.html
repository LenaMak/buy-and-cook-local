Potato Dumplings

It's a traditional side dish that can be served with almost any meal. You can also add some bacon if you feel up to it. Easy to cook and tastes delicious.

prep time

30 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

2 servings

Tags : potato egg butter

instructions

step 1

Cook potatoes in their skins one day before or early in the same day, let them cool slightly and peel them. Mash or rice the potatoes and put them in the fridge until they get cold or leave them overnight.

step 2

Make croutons by cutting bread into cubes.

step 3

Melt butter in the pan over medium heat, add bread cubes and fry. Stir frequently until they become brown on all sides, set aside to let them cool.

step 4

Take the dough from the fridge and put it into a bowl, add potato starch, an egg and salt. Mix together with your hands until the dough sticks together, divide it into portions. If the mixture is too moist, add more starch.

step 5

Form 6-7 dumplings adding a few croutons in the middle of each dumpling. Wet your hands with water or apply some flour on your palms to avoid dumplings stick to your hands.

step 6

Boil water, put in salt and drop dumplings gently. Simmer them for about 15-20 minutes without a cover.

step 7

Remove dumplings with a slotted spoon and serve.

ingredients

600 gr potatoes cooked in their skins

2 slices day-old bread

14 gr butter

100 gr potato starch

1 large egg

3 gr salt

tools

Cooking Pot With Lid

Measuring Spoons

Slotted Spoon

Pan
